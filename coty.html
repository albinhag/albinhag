Coty, the owner of Wella, wanted us to figure out how to bring millenials to hairdressing services. This was a brief where we, a group of students that'd never met before, were to come up with a concept and explain it in a video in less than 40 hours.


As one can expect, the process was very intense.


I personally was given the task to put together the actual movie which was stressful, but very rewarding. A tight deadline may force you to work rapidly, but being satisfied with the result after such a task is extremely worthwile. Plus, the client loved it as well!


Tools:

Sketch, Adobe Photoshop, Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro X.
