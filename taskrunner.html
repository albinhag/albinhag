I got the opportunity to work with taskrunner.se, a startup based in Stockholm, where the goal was to take a deeper look into their visitors' online behaviors.


I started this module by getting certified in Google Analytics, and later on proceeded together with my project group to analyze and optimize taskrunner's website based on their data.


Together we set up funnels, crunched numbers, and put together a comprehensive report with suggestions on how they could optimize their site - 14-pages filled with excellent advice!


Tools:

Google Analytics, HotJar, Google Tag Manager.
