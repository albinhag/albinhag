médecins sans frontières


What does every non-profit organization want except for world peace? Donations of course!


This was another one of those "come up with a concept + create video in less than 40 hours"-type of task, where the brief was to secure more doners to MSF since they stopped accepting funds from the EU as a protest against restricted refugee-policies.


Again, I was in charge of putting the video together. Maybe not the most solid idea, but what we lacked in concept, we compensated with the cuteness of our video...


Tools:

Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro X, Pens, papers & scissors (analog).
