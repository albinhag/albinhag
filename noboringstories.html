noboringstories.com


Got a couple of minutes to kill? Are you bored? Is reading your preferred style of consuming entartainment? Look no further! I present to you: noboringstories.com!


This is a service me and three fellow Hyper Island students built together which presents a completely random short story in a nice and readble format for every device.


This is done by scraping the most upvoted stories from one of reddit's biggest subs, r/WritingPrompts, and presenting it as a webapp. This way we bypass the relatively big friction between reddit and non-reddit users, so everyone can enjoy the glorious content of r/WritingPromtps!


Tools:

Git, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Reddit API, Adobe Illustrator.
